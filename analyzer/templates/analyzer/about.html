About the Project

Bridging the gap between academic learning and industry requirements.

The Problem

Many diploma and engineering students graduate with theoretical knowledge but lack the specific practical skills required by employers. This creates a "skill gap" that makes it difficult for fresh graduates to secure internships and jobs in their desired fields.

Our Solution

The Internship Skill Gap Analyzer helps students systematically identify these missing skills. By selecting their academic branch and comparing their current skillset with the requirements of specific job roles, students receive a personalized roadmap. We provide targeted resources like tutorial links and reference materials to help students bridge this gap efficiently.
